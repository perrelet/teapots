Shualee Cook's play (world premiere 18 September 2024, Slightly Askew Theatre
Ensemble, St. Louis) drops a 21-year-old Alice Liddell back into the Mad Tea
Party, where Lewis Carroll himself sends her to read Shakespeare's *The Tempest* —
turning the tea table into a philosophical treasure hunt drawing parallels to
Miranda and Ariel. The `mashes-up` edge to [[mad-tea-party]] is the point: the
tea table is the fulcrum where two canonical worlds fuse (the Shakespeare side has
no teapot entry to link). Distinct from [[tempest-in-a-teapot-idiom]] — same name,
different phenomenon.
