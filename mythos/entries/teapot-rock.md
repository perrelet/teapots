The literal teapot at the root of the whole "Teapot Dome" name: a sandstone
formation in Natrona County, Wyoming, whose eroded profile once suggested a spout
and a handle — a teapot *found*, not made, by weather and the human eye. The
same phenomenon that [[sagittarius-teapot]] performs on stars, performed on rock.

Its name propagated outward — to the naval oil reserve beneath it and, from
there, to the [[teapot-dome-scandal]] — a `namesake` chain in which only the
*word* travelled, never the teapot. This is the central teapot of the trio; the
scandal is not.
