(Nietzsche runs "Heaven, Inc." and schemes to fire God) skewers religion in a
Douglas-Adams vein — but verification found **no teapot in the book at all**: the
word appears only in the title, as a wink at [[russells-teapot]]. So it **fails
Perrelet's centrality gate** — the teapot is not the tenant, only a `title-only`
allusion — and is kept, like [[teapot-dome-scandal]], to show the gate working.
