A palace housekeeper enchanted into a teapot, Mrs. Potts is the warm, maternal
centre of the Beast's cursed household — she pours, she consoles, she mothers
(the chipped cup Chip is her son). Her `char:arc` is not moral growth but a
**curse-restoration** shape: teapot by enchantment, human again when the spell
breaks. A teapot as caregiver — `hospitality` made flesh — and the clearest case
of the vessel standing for nurture itself.
