A hiking trail near Chilliwack where, over years, hikers have hidden and left more
than a hundred teapots and teacups along the route, so that finding them becomes
the point of the walk. A `collection` with no collector — a distributed,
community-made hoard on the landscape — and quietly `hospitality`: strangers
leaving teapots for strangers to find.
