An animated retelling of the genie myth set in modern Shanghai, in which the
wish-granting dragon Long is imprisoned not in a lamp but in an ornate **teapot** —
a deliberate cultural relocation of the Aladdin story onto a Chinese vessel. The
teapot is the plot's central magical object: rubbed, summoned from, bargained
with. It answers, on screen, a question raised in the source chat — *is the lamp
the genie emerges from a sort of teapot?* Here it simply is one, taking its place
in the family of magical vessels.
