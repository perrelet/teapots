A young couple find a magical brass teapot that fills with cash whenever they
experience pain — so they hurt themselves, then others, to get rich. A dark fairy
tale in which the teapot is the engine of the plot: a `trickster` object that
tests how far people will go, and a `transformation` that runs the wrong way, from
love toward greed. The teapot as tempter rather than nurturer — the shadow of
[[andersens-teapot]].
