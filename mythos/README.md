# Mythos

A faceted taxonomy of the teapot as a central object of human meaning-making —
the working corpus behind the Fractal Teapot. Every place the teapot surfaces as
the *tenant* of a work, tagged across independent axes so the collection can be
re-sorted rather than pre-sorted.

- **[SCHEMA.md](SCHEMA.md)** — facet axes, vocabularies, the three governing rules
  (centrality gate · split-vs-shift · primary-register tie-break), the typed link
  graph, the Character sub-schema, and the two hypotheses under test.
- **[entries/](entries/)** — one file per teapot, faceted in frontmatter.
- **[views/](views/)** — generated cross-sections (rebuild with `python build-views.py`):
  [hypotheses](views/hypotheses.md) · [by-ontology](views/by-ontology.md) ·
  [by-mode](views/by-mode.md) · [by-spout](views/by-spout.md) · [by-domain](views/by-domain.md).

## Early hypothesis signals (n=36)

- **Spout hypothesis** — now 14 `spout: yes` entries, still clustering as
  predicted: `material` + `duality`/`mechanism` + physics + embodiment + the
  pouring ceremonies (assassin, Cadogan, teapot effect, chocolate, Little Teapot,
  teapotting, the Moroccan and Turkish pours, the found-form rock & asterism, the
  emoji). The `conceptual`/`refusal` teapots (Russell, Perrelet, 418) are
  `spout: no`. The vessel-that-gives-out reading is holding strongly.
- **Self-reference hypothesis** — the self-naming cluster has grown to **four**
  (HTTP 418, "I'm a Little Teapot", the teapot emoji 🫖, teapotting), linked by the
  shared `self-naming` tag — the strange-loop nucleus the whole braid may hang on.
- **Service-vs-refusal (character arc)** — Andersen (service) ↔ Bunbuku (refusal),
  with Mrs. Potts (curse-restoration) and Blyton (none) filling out `char:arc`.
- **Genealogy** — everything descends from [Russell](entries/russells-teapot.md):
  Perrelet **responds-to**, Gong & Operation Cosmic Teapot **allude-to**. Russell
  has no outgoing edges — the root everyone points at.

## Citations verified (2026-07-04)

All 13 `needs-check` entries were web-verified, corrected, and set to
`confidence: verified`. Notable fixes: Edenton was ten months *after* Boston (not
before); Trenton holds *veilleuses* (night-light teapots); Teapot Dome Station was
built by Jack Ainsworth and relocated in 2012; Sky Kingdom was in Besut (not Hulu
Terengganu); Carolyn Lee Boyd is New England-based; "Tao in a Teapot" is by
Alexander Thomas (2020). **Operation Cosmic Teapot** moved to the contrast set —
no teapot in the book, title-only allusion, fails the centrality gate.
